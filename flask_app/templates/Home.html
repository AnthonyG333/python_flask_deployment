From the Owner

Lincoln St. Cafe brings insight into life in Sicily through its traditional Italian cuisine. In order to create the truest sense of authentic Italian flavors we source all ingredients from local organic farms. We take pride in our belief that there is no length to which we will not go in order to bring the freshest and most delectable dishes to our customers.
